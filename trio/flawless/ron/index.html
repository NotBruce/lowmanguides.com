STRATEGY

This encounter is very quick and easy. Assign one player to solo Nodes while the other two work together to kill Tormentors in order to extend the timer.

A useful tip is to leave a Tormentor alive for the next phase, then kill the Tormentor when the timer starts. This way, you can get an instant tune extension on the next round.

Repeat this four times and you win.

STRATEGY

Start by grabbing the buff for both nodes then well-skate to the opposite side.

Once you complete your first node, you two will then do the buff for that side, allowing you to activate the next node on the same side without needing to cross again. After you both activate your second node, go back and receive your buff again then cross sides again.

After both of you complete your nodes, grab your buff and repeat what I described above. Shoot the same node on your side then grab your buff again, then cross sides for the final node on that floor.

As for dealing with the adds, simply clear one side at a time to be safe then go up and repeat the same exact thing two more times. You can also just cross every time to make this simpler, but it makes the encounter slightly more annoying.

Boss Health: Unknown.

       Submission                     Any Solar                     Gjallarhorn

 Just spam Fusion grenades and rockets.

STRATEGY

Spawn kill the adds as fast as possible to spawn the Centurions quicker. Killing the Centurions will always spawn the Colossuses which grant you Planetary Insight. The easiest way to approach the callouts for this encounter is by having one player scout for each side, and type the first callout for TOP and the second callout for BOTTOM. For example, “RL” means that top would be Right and bottom would be Left.

Two players should do both top planets and the other player should do bottom as they’re closer. Once you have both callouts, simply swap the planets around. For the player doing bottom Solo, he must take his planet from one side to the other, deposit it, then pick up the respective planet and return it back to his original side.

Once the inners reveal themselves, deposit the correct planets on the inner plates then prepare for DPS.

DAMAGE PHASE

Wait until the boss is glowing to step on any plates. Keep in mind that the first plate will always be one of the 2 matching planets: If there are 2 light and 1 dark, it will start on Light and vice versa.

Additionally, the plates will rotate between each color and never repeat, meaning:

light→dark→light OR  dark→light→dark

Just spam rockets and Fusion grenades then repeat this until he’s dead. Final stand is pretty chill.

Boss Health: 12,000,000M~

Debuff

               

         Succession                    Any Trace                    Gjallarhorn

                                                                                     

Activate Pack Hunter before you hit the Debuff finisher.

STRATEGY

Start by having two players do Dark and Light nodes. The other player will focus on getting stuns to stop Nezarec’s Hatred. After that, the player will stun the shoulders to reveal which Refuge needs to be created.

Create the refuge and refresh it once his wings begin glowing. After that, complete the both nodes and prepare your preferred Colossus to be finishered for the debuff. The player with Felwinter’s Helm should wait to do the finisher until he sees the beam begin starting on Nezarec’s “prison”.

Do the finisher then do as much damage as possible. Shoot the ground with your rockets when he lifts up in the air with your rockets. After that, grab as much ammo as possible and get ready to do it again.

Keep in mind that Refuges will be need to be created sooner on the second and third phases as the boss activates his wipe ability faster on later phases.

Final stand is very easy, just rocket him and you’ll win.
